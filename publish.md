## 应用描述

### 简短描述 (170字符内)

**English:**

**中文:**

---

### 详细描述

**English:**

Osmo Controller is a professional mobile app that connects to your DJI Osmo camera via Bluetooth, giving you full control of your device.

**Key Features:**
- **Camera Control**: Start/stop recording, take photos, switch between video and photo modes
- **Real-time Status**: Monitor battery level, storage capacity, recording time, and device temperature
- **GPS Integration**: Capture and push GPS coordinates to your camera for location-tagged footage
- **Mode Selection**: Quick access to video, photo, slow motion, and timelapse modes
- **Multiple Languages**: Supports English, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), and Japanese

Perfect for content creators, vloggers, and anyone who wants to get the most out of their DJI Osmo device.

**Supported Devices:**
- DJI Osmo Action 4
- DJI Osmo Action 5 Pro
- DJI Osmo Action 6
- DJI Osmo 360

**中文:**

Osmo 遥控器是一款专业的移动应用，通过蓝牙连接您的 DJI Osmo 相机，让您完全掌控您的设备。

**主要功能：**
- **相机控制**：开始/停止录制、拍摄照片、在视频和照片模式之间切换
- **实时状态**：监控电量、存储容量、录制时间和设备温度
- **GPS 集成**：捕获并推送 GPS 坐标到您的相机，实现位置标记
- **模式选择**：快速访问视频、照片、慢动作和延时摄影模式
- **多语言支持**：支持英语、中文（简体和繁体）和日语

非常适合内容创作者、Vlog 博主和任何想要充分利用 DJI Osmo 设备的用户。

**支持设备：**
- DJI Osmo Action 4
- DJI Osmo Action 5 Pro
- DJI Osmo Action 6
- DJI Osmo 360
